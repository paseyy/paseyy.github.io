Computer Vision and AI Projects

FitnessPose

Launched during my semester abroad in Pohang, my team and I created a tool for identifying mistakes in workout form. We used a toolchain consisting of ViTPose and a slightly modified version of PoseConv3D. We trained and tested our approach on the Fitness-AQA dataset, where we improve the baseline accuracy by about 7%. As of July 2025, our approach is still the state of the art on Fitness-AQA.

Rubik's Cube Solver

We use traditional (= no machine learning) computer vision pipelines to detect and digitize a Rubik's Cube configuration. Afterwards, we use the Twophase solver to find a solution for the cube and provide detailed instructions via a 3D user interface. We were surprised at the accuracy and speed with which the rule-based CV pipeline is able complete this task.
